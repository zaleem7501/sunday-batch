What to roast: Use whatever needs to be used up. The technique stays the same — high heat, single layer, don't crowd. Almost anything edible becomes better roasted.

Freezing: Roasted vegetables freeze reasonably well, though texture softens. Broccolini and sweet potato hold up best. Cherry tomatoes become sauce-like when frozen — useful for different purposes.
