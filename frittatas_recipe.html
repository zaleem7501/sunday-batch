Storage
Fridge (4 days): Cool completely. Line an airtight container with paper towel, arrange in single layer, lid on.

Freezer (3 months): Flash freeze on a baking sheet 1 hour until solid, then transfer to a container. Reheat from frozen at 50% power 60–90 seconds.

Key: Always remove from silicone liners before storing — liners trap condensation and make the bottoms soggy.
